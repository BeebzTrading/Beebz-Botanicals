Our Brand Story

Beebz Botanicals is a South African self‑care brand inspired by the country’s diverse landscapes, botanical heritage and coastal environments. Our collection celebrates everyday self‑care through botanical soaps, luxury soap accessories and curated bath ritual kits.

Each soap in our range is produced locally in KwaZulu‑Natal using nourishing plant oils and carefully blended fragrances inspired by South African florals and coastal scents. Wrapped in eco‑friendly paper packaging, our products are designed to bring moments of calm and indulgence to everyday routines.

At B.Z Trading, we believe thoughtfully crafted botanical products can transform everyday routines into moments of calm and wellbeing.
